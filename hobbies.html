I’ve always been a creative soul, spending countless hours on my computer experimenting and designing. Whether I’m creating schedules, drafting documents, or writing stories, there’s just something fulfilling about bringing my ideas to life. This passion led me to start my own little business where I design journals and notebooks, each one reflecting my style and vision.

Design is my jam, and it doesn’t stop there! I love diving into interior design, finding the perfect colors and arrangements to make a space feel just right. Playing The Sims is another fun outlet for my creativity—I get to design dream homes and explore different styles without any limitations.

When I’m not busy with projects, I enjoy hanging out with friends, whether it’s checking out new restaurants or whipping up something delicious at home. I also love to travel whenever I can, soaking up new cultures and gathering inspiration along the way. Hiking is another passion of mine. There’s nothing quite like being outdoors, exploring nature, and enjoying the fresh air while enjoying the creative works of the greatest designer.

Recently, I’ve picked up web content creation as a new hobby, diving into the world of web development. Learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript has opened up a whole new realm of creativity for me, allowing me to craft cool and functional websites.

Each of these hobbies keeps me inspired and excited, blending my love for creativity with a bit of adventure in everyday life.
